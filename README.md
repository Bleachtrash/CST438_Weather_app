**CST348 Weather App**
-

**Features**
- Sign In Page & Sign Up Page
- Weather View Page
  - Defaults to your current location.
  - Shows current temperature, current weather, and upcomming hourly weather.
- Favorite Locations Page
  - Location sarch bar.
  - Clickable locations that go to that location's Weather View page.

**How To Use**
- Create an account -- passwords must be at least 6 characters long.
- Look at weather for your current location.
- Click the heart button in the top right to access "Favorite Locations" Page.
- Click the edit button in the "Favorite Locations" page to search for and add locations or delete existing favorite locations.
- Click the back button to return to your current location, or click on a favorite location to see the weather there.
- When you're done click the logout button in the top right.
